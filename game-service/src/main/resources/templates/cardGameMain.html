WHAT’S ASTERION ?

Asterion Battle is an unforgettable heroic adventure. Step into the role of a fearless warrior and descend into shadowy, treacherous dungeons to face Asterion, the fearsome monster holding the princess captive. Armed with your blade and unshakable courage, you’ll confront challenges that only a true hero can conquer. Are you ready to save the princess and become a legend?

Character introduction

A monstrous figure with blazing red eyes and wings like shadows of despair. Asterion is pure terror, ruling the labyrinth with brute strength and cunning. Few have faced him—and none have survived.
